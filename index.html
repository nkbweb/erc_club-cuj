About ERC Club

Our mission is to foster a deep understanding and passion for electronics and robotics through hands-on learning, collaboration, and innovation. We are a community of curious minds and technology enthusiasts dedicated to exploring the frontiers of technology, building exciting projects, and advancing our skills together.

Events

1. Basic Electronics Workshop: Introduction to circuits, components, and basic soldering techniques

2.Arduino & Raspberry Pi Projects: Hands-on sessions for creating innovative projects using these popular platforms.

3.Robotics 101: Building and programming simple robots, ideal for beginners.

Proudly affiliated with IEEE.

Our club is proud to be affiliated with the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE), the world’s largest professional association for advancing technology. This affiliation provides our members with access to cutting-edge resources, industry-leading research, and a global network of professionals.
